02 — The Studio
About the house

A design house for the modern motorcycle.

We are a small, obsessive studio that treats every motorcycle as a piece of moving sculpture. Founded in 2023 in Kaunas, Lithuania, we blend old-world craftsmanship with digital engineering — clay, steel and pixels in the same breath.

From a napkin sketch to a road-ready prototype, we own every surface, every weld line and every reflection. The result is machines that feel inevitable — like they could only ever have looked this way.
