Who we are

Small workshop.
Industrial standards.

Plieno Pulsas is an independent metalworking shop in the Vilnius region, founded in 2024 on a simple belief: that precision and honesty never go out of style.

We pair traditional craftsmanship with modern CNC equipment to cut, bend, cast, weld and finish steel, stainless and aluminium — from one-off custom pieces to repeat production runs. Every weld is inspected, every edge is deburred, and every deadline is treated as a promise.

  • Certified welding across MIG/MAG, TIG and MMA processes.
  • Fiber-laser cutting and CNC bending for tight tolerances.
  • Transparent quoting and a top-tier (A) credit rating.
